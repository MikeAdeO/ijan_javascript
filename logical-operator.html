Comparison Operator

  • == equal to
  • === equal value and equal type
  • != not equal to
  • !== not equal to value and not to equal type
  • > greater than
  • < less than
  • >= greater than or equal to
  • <= less than or equal to
  • ? ternary operator

LOGICAL OPERATORS

T - TRUE

F - FALSE

  • && Logical AND TT
  • || LOGICAL OR TF, FT
  • ! LOGICAL NOT
